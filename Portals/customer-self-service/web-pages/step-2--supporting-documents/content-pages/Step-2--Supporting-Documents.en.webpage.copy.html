10. Supporting documents

Important notes about documents to be uploaded:

  1. UPLOADED DOCUMENTS MUST BE LESS THAN 5 MB and meet accepted formats: jpg, gif, png, pdf, doc, docx, txt, xlsx.
  2. SAVE your application before uploading your documents
  3. Uploaded documents over 5 MB will result in loss of your application data.

After clicking "Submit", please wait for the confirmation page. Uploading documents may take time depending on the file size. Thank you for your patience
